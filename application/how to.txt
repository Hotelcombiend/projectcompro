First add product to stock by pressing 'S' to go to "Stock" menu and choose "Purchase New Product" to add product
in "Purchase New Product" fill in the details of your product.

Next when you sale your product, simply choose "Sale Product" then enter the product ID and enter bill number, customer name, quantity
then press "Enter".

If you want to check bill, then go to "Bill" menu and enter bill number.

If you want to check the profit, then go to "Report" menu and choose Profit report.
